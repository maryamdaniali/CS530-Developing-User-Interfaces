About US

This application is a part of final project for CS530, Developing User Interfaces course, offered at Computer Science Department, at Drexel University.

Here, we are going to offer you an application which can solve these issues: It is going to suggest you what to cook based on the feature set you have in your mind, whether it is the price, the healthiness, fast cooking, etc.

You can also use the search feature, to find the recipe you like, by filtering its name of its features, then you can select among the list provided

You can also mark a recipe as one of your favorite ones, to return back to them in futher, although you can de-favorite a recipe whenever you want on its page.
